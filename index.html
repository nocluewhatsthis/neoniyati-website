NeoNiyati

Legal Hiring. Focused on long-term fit.

NeoNiyati is a legal talent advisory working with in-house teams and law firms on hiring, primarily at mid to senior levels.

We focus on helping organizations make better hiring decisions by identifying candidates who are a strong match in terms of capability, intent, and culture.

What we do

We work on legal hiring across in-house teams and law firms, with a focus on roles where the right fit matters.

Our approach is simple — understand the role well, and share a small set of relevant, well-matched candidates.

How we work

Quality over volume. We do not send multiple profiles. Instead, we focus on a smaller, well-considered shortlist.

Beyond the CV. Candidates are evaluated on experience, intent, communication, and overall fit.

Long-term thinking. The focus is not just on closing a role, but on building the right team.

Who we work with

In-house legal teams and law firms looking to hire at mid to senior levels.

About the founder

NeoNiyati is founded by Lavanya Behl, who brings over 15 years of experience, including a decade in the legal industry.

She has worked at Amarchand Mangaldas and later co-founded LawWiser, where she worked closely with in-house leaders and law firms across the ecosystem.
